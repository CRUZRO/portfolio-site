Palladium

Introduction

Most music apps only offer the popular or latest Salsa and Latin songs or the same well-known artists, making it hard to discover variety. Palladium caters to Salsa artists, dancers, and Latin music lovers who struggle finding classic hits not found in common music apps.

My Process

Objective

Create a music library capturing the evolution of Salsa and Latin music and inspire upcoming artists as well as promote their music.

User flows

The key features of this app are:

  1. Creating an account
  2. Searching and playing for Latin music
  3. Creating and editing playlists

Testing

I designed two screens for the onboarding process but some of the users didn't understand the origin of the app's name. For this reason, I added an extra screen explaining the definition of the word.

When I first styled the screen, they had a monochromatic color palette with bright greens, but users weren’t pleased with the look. Therefore, I searched for colors that would relate to the theme and implemented a red, blue, and green palette since many Latin countries have those colors in their flags.

I had placed the logo on top of each screen. After receiving feedback, I included each section’s title to give the user more context. Also, I kept a flying “play” button for both versions.

Conclusion

This project was challenging since learning both guidelines (Human Interface and Material Design). In general, I was better at managing my time, setting goals, and overall handling AdobeXD. I had a setback when picking the brand colors, but managed to find a more suitable color palette. Also, I enjoyed testing the prototypes since it gave me a wider panorama of the product’s usability and design aspects to improve.
